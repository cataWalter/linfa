Linfa is your personal houseplant assistant. Track your plants, set care reminders, and watch them grow over time. Completely free, open source, and privacy-first.

## Why Linfa?

Houseplants need regular care: watering, fertilizing, repotting, leaf cleaning. It's easy to forget when you last watered a specific plant, especially when you have several.

Existing apps like Planta, Blossom, and PictureThis are freemium: useful features are behind paywalls (€25-30/year), require accounts, and your data stays on their servers.

Linfa is different:
- **100% free** - no paywalls, no subscriptions
- **Open source** - code is transparent, anyone can contribute
- **Privacy-first** - all data stays on your device, no account required
- **Offline-first** - works without internet connection
- **Simple** - does one thing and does it well

## Features

### Core
- Add plants with photos and basic information
- Customizable reminders for each plant
- Local push notifications - no server needed
- Growth timeline with photos
- Notes and observations for each plant

### Organization
- Group by room or location
- Filter by plant type
- Search by name
- Sort by last check or name

### Export
- Export data to JSON
- Export photos to gallery
- Manual backup to personal cloud

### Customization
- Icons for reminder types
- Colors for plant status
- Customizable notification sounds
- Light/dark theme

## Plant Database

Linfa includes a base database of common plants with care advice:

| Plant | Watering | Light | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|-------|
| Pothos | Every 7-10 days | Indirect | Very resilient |
| Monstera | Every 7 days | Bright indirect | Loves humidity |
| Sansevieria | Every 14-21 days | Any | Almost indestructible |
| Ficus | Every 7-10 days | Bright indirect | Sensitive to changes |
| Succulents | Every 14-21 days | Direct | Little water |
| Fern | Every 3-5 days | Indirect | Loves humidity |

## Privacy

Linfa stores all data locally on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Your plant data belongs to you and stays with you.
